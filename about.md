# Music Downloader

Music Downloader automatically downloads the songs and audio assets required by a Geometry Dash level when you download the level.

Normally, some levels require you to manually open the audio assets menu and download missing songs or SFX before playing. This mod automates that step by triggering Geometry Dash's native multi-asset download flow directly from the level info screen.

## Features

- Automatically downloads the main custom song when downloading a level.
- Automatically downloads additional 2.2 music library songs.
- Automatically downloads missing SFX assets.
- Uses Geometry Dash's native download logic, so the normal song info box and download progress can still be used.
- Designed to stay unobtrusive: download the level, let the mod handle the audio assets.

## Usage

1. Download a level.
2. The mod automatically starts downloading the required audio assets.
3. Once the assets are downloaded, the level should be ready to play with its intended audio.

## Notes

Large levels can use many audio assets, so downloading everything may still take a moment depending on your connection and Geometry Dash's servers.
